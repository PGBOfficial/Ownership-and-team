# Mehrazan Data Develop Yazd

The **Persian Gulf Blockchain (PGB)** project is owned and developed by **Mehrazan Data Develop Yazd**.  
This company is responsible for developing the **first Layer-1 blockchain in Iran** with a focus on the cultural–sports sector.

## Founder and Board of Directors
- **Enayat Shokuhian** – Founder of the company, Chairman of the Board, and Founder of the Persian Gulf Blockchain project  
- **Engineer Ali Dehghani** – CEO of the company and shareholder  
- **Engineer Mohammad Reza Barahani** – Vice Chairman of the Board and project advisor  
- **Dr. Meysam Ebrahimi** – Board member and university professor  

## Development Team
- The official **Persian Gulf Blockchain (PGB)** team is responsible for network and ecosystem development.
